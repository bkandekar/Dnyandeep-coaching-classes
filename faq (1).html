Got Questions?

Common Queries Answered

We transparently lay out all academic, commercial, and operational policies to help local families make well-informed decisions.

How can I secure admission for my child at Dnyandeep Coaching Classes?
You can initiate the process by visiting our campus near Laxmi Biroba Mandir, Savarde, or filling out the inquiry form on our Contact page. Our academic counselor will guide you through an eligibility analysis and recommend the most suitable batch.
What are the typical class timings for school board batches?
School board foundation programs (Classes 8th to 10th) are scheduled in two shifts: Morning (7:00 AM - 9:30 AM) and Evening (5:00 PM - 7:30 PM). This allows students from Savarde and nearby schools to join without experiencing any school time conflicts.
Do you allow coaching fee payments in installments?
Yes, absolutely. To support families in the Hatkanangale and Kolhapur rural communities, we offer flexible, interest-free fee structures. For our yearly board foundation programs, parents can split the fees into 2 to 4 convenient installments payable across the term.
Can students attend demo classes before finalizing their admission?
Yes, we provide a 3-day free trial demo pass to all new aspirants. This allows students to experience our interactive lecturing style, inspect our study halls, and interface with our faculties before committing to the full-term programs.
Are study materials and revision cards included in the fees?
Yes, all students receive comprehensive printed chapter books, short-cut index revision cards, question banks, and Sunday test series booklets free of any extra cost. The cost is fully bundled with the initial course fee.
How often do you conduct mock examinations?
We follow a strict assessment calendar. We conduct topic-wise diagnostic tests every Sunday, followed by detailed monthly full-unit exams. Prior to board or government exams, we also host three rounds of full-syllabus pre-board mocks.
How do you handle student doubts and custom concept clearance?
We have a dedicated "Doubt Clearance Desk" active daily from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM. Students can sit face-to-face with our professors to resolve challenging problems or clarify advanced concepts individually.
Do you provide online classes or live hybrid support?
We are primarily an elite offline coaching institute to ensure maximum discipline and face-to-face focus. However, we do provide live-hybrid video backup links of missed core lectures for students who are absent due to medical emergencies.
What are the qualifications of the teaching staff at Dnyandeep?
Our academic faculty comprises seasoned, highly qualified subject experts holding postgraduate and doctoral degrees in Science, Mathematics, and Humanities, with over 10+ years of professional mentoring experience.
What is the average student batch size in a classroom?
To guarantee individual attention and close tracking, we restrict our regular board batches to a maximum of 35-40 students per class, and competitive batches to 30 scholars. This ensures teacher-student interactions remain high.
Do you send progress reports and test marks to parents?
Yes, absolutely. We use an automated SMS gateway to send instant mock test scores, attendance logs, and student feedback summaries to parents' registered mobile numbers within 24 hours of each Sunday test.
What is the refund policy if a student wishes to withdraw?
Fees once paid are non-refundable. However, in special cases involving serious medical emergencies or family relocation outside the Kolhapur district, a proportional adjustment can be approved by the management.
How does the institute handle public holidays and student leaves?
We observe major national holidays, and any scheduled class cancellations are notified to parents and students at least 48 hours in advance. If a student is on personal leave, they must submit a formal parent slip.
Do you provide dedicated transport facilities for remote students?
While we do not operate our own buses, our Savarde campus is located near central transport corridors, and we help coordinate safe group-sharing transport vans with verified local drivers for remote students.
Who can I contact if I have further inquiries or issues?
You can directly connect with our administrative desk by calling +91 83299 31123 or messaging us on our verified WhatsApp line. We are open for inquiries from 8:00 AM to 8:30 PM.
