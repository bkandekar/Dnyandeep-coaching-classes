Privacy Policy

Home / Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

At Dnyandeep Coaching Classes, accessible from our Savarde premises or via our web assets, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our students and visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by us and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal info such as student name, guardian details, contact mobile number, academic grade, and historical marks when registering or filling our enquiry cards. This is critical to conduct assessment tests and keep parents updated.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our educational classes.
  • Analyze academic progress and customize student training models.
  • Send automated attendance alerts and test mark sheets to parents.
  • Process admission application forms and installment schedules.

3. Consent and Security

By using our services or website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. We implement industrial-grade security measures to keep all scholar lists completely confidential.
