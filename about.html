Since 2014

The Legacy of Dnyandeep

Dnyandeep Coaching Classes was founded in 2014 by a team of highly passionate educators with a core desire to bridge the gap between academic textbooks and conceptual clarity. Located near Laxmi Biroba Mandir, Savarde, our coaching academy is not just a commercial tutorial center—it is a learning laboratory that fosters intellectual growth.

Over the past 12 years, we have empowered more than 5,000 students from Savarde, Hatkanangale, and neighboring areas to master challenging syllabus patterns, boost scores, and win top government jobs in competitive environments.

Our teaching style centers strictly around "active cognitive involvement" rather than plain rote-memorization. By encouraging inquiry, developing mental reasoning models, and implementing strict mock schedules, we guarantee success to every sincere candidate.

Our Vision

To establish Dnyandeep Coaching Classes as an institution of peerless trust, generating scholars, thinkers, and ethical leaders who contribute dynamically to national progress through academic mastery.

Our Mission

To deliver modern, student-centric, and exceptionally clear conceptual frameworks, ensuring maximum learning optimization while keeping education extremely affordable and accessible for rural communities.

Our History

A Journey of Progress

A quick retrospective look into the major growth checkpoints of Dnyandeep Classes since our foundation.

2014

Humble Beginnings

Dnyandeep Classes opens its doors with just 1 classroom, 2 teachers, and a small batch of 15 student candidates focusing strictly on secondary board exams.

2017

Academic Infrastructure Expansion

We expand into our current Savarde premises near Laxmi Biroba Mandir, adding multiple smart-enabled lecture halls, a dedicated doubt room, and library sections.

2020

Competitive Programs Rollout

By recruiting senior specialists, we introduce dedicated training pathways for central government job exams like SSC CGL, Banking (IBPS/SBI), and RRB.

2023

Record-Breaking Board Results

Achieved a stellar milestone with 100% passing rates in class 10 boards and multiple students breaching the elite 98% tier across Hatkanangale Taluka.

2026

Hybrid Classroom Integration

Equipped with full digital cameras to stream lectures live and provide seamless web records, ensuring offline classes are completely backstopped by cloud access.
